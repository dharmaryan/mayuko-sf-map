Quick Tips
Keep your phone in your pocket when walking — phone snatching is common citywide
Never leave anything visible in a parked car — break-ins happen everywhere, even “safe” areas
Uber/Lyft at night if crossing through sketchy areas
Trust your gut — if a street feels off, turn around
Your area (Cathedral Hill) borders the Tenderloin to the east — head west toward Japantown or south toward Hayes Valley instead
